Getting Started

  1. Upload your dataset
    Choose a CSV or Excel file containing your data. The file should have headers in the first row.
  2. Explore your data
    After upload, you'll see basic information about your dataset including the number of rows and columns.
  3. Visualize your data
    Navigate to the Visualization page to create charts and graphs. Select columns, chart types, and apply filters as needed.
  4. Analyze your data
    Go to the Analysis page to view statistical summaries, missing value information, and correlation matrices.
  5. Export your results
    Download your data or analysis results in CSV, Excel, or JSON formats using the export buttons.
